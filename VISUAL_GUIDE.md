## 🎬 User Journey

### New User Journey
```
1. Land on Home Page
   ↓
2. See all listings (public)
   ↓
3. Click "Sign Up"
   ↓
4. Fill registration form
   ↓
5. Auto-login after signup
   ↓
6. Redirected to Home
   ↓
7. Click "Add Listing"
   ↓
8. Fill listing form + upload image
   ↓
9. Listing created
   ↓
10. View own listing
```

### Existing User Journey
```
1. Land on Home Page
   ↓
2. Click "Login"
   ↓
3. Enter credentials
   ↓
4. Redirected to Home
   ↓
5. Browse listings
   ↓
6. Click on a listing
   ↓
7. View details
   ↓
8. Add review with rating
   ↓
9. Review appears instantly
```

### Owner Journey
```
1. Login
   ↓
2. View own listing
   ↓
3. Click "Edit Listing"
   ↓
4. Update details
   ↓
5. Save changes
   ↓
6. View updated listing
   ↓
7. Or click "Delete Listing"
   ↓
8. Confirm deletion
   ↓
9. Redirected to Home
```
